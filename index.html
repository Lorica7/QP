About Me

Danielle LaMorte

Front-end Web Developer

I am a web developer who simply loves creating beautiful websites and great user experiences. I studied fullstack web development at UPenn's coding bootcamp, but I focus on front-end coding, and website creation. With an undergraduate degree in English, I have communicative strength that shows in client interaction and in the sites I make.

Strong abilities in coding, editing and copywriting, mean I have everything that is needed to communicate effectively online through websites. WordPress, React, Javascript, are my favorite tools and areas of focus.

Outside of work, I code more and play with the Adobe Creative Suite. I also hang out with my dogs, practice yoga and chase the green flash in Naples, Florida.
